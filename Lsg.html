Lucknow Super Giants (LSG)



Lucknow Super Giants represents Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh.



The franchise joined the IPL in 2022.



LSG quickly became a competitive side and reached the playoffs in its early seasons.



The team's home ground is Bharat Ratna Shri Atal Bihari Vajpayee Ekana Cricket Stadium.



The franchise continues to build a strong team for future success.
