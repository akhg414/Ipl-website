Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH)



Sunrisers Hyderabad represents Hyderabad, Telangana.



The franchise joined the IPL in 2013 and quickly became a competitive team.



SRH is famous for strong bowling attacks and disciplined performances.



The team's home ground is the Rajiv Gandhi International Cricket Stadium.



The team won its first IPL title in 2016.
