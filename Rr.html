Rajasthan Royals (RR)



Rajasthan Royals represents the state of Rajasthan.



The team won the very first IPL season in 2008.



RR is known for giving opportunities to young and talented players.



The franchise has built a reputation for smart team planning and player development.



The team's home ground is the Sawai Mansingh Stadium in Jaipur.
