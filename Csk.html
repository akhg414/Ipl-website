Chennai Super Kings (CSK)



Chennai Super Kings is one of the most successful teams in IPL history. The team represents Chennai, Tamil Nadu, and was founded in 2008.



CSK is famous for its consistency and strong performances. The team has reached the playoffs many times and won multiple IPL titles.



MS Dhoni is the most iconic player and captain of the franchise. His leadership has played a major role in the team's success.



The home ground of CSK is the M. A. Chidambaram Stadium, also known as Chepauk Stadium.



Fans proudly call themselves the Yellow Army and support the team passionately.
