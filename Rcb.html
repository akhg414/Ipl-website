Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB)



Royal Challengers Bengaluru represents Bengaluru, Karnataka.



The team is one of the most popular IPL franchises and has a huge fan following.



Virat Kohli has been the face of RCB for many years and is one of the greatest players in IPL history.



RCB is known for exciting batting performances and thrilling matches.



The team's home ground is the M. Chinnaswamy Stadium.
